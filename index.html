The Traditional System Of Agriculture Has Been In Practice For More Than Ten Thousand Years, And Has Been Recognized As More Sustainable Than Any Other System Of Agriculture. In Course Of Time, During The Late Sixties Of The Last Century, When The India Faced Shortage Of Food Grains To Feed The Ever Increasing Population, Green Revolution Was Launched, Which Led To A Significant Increase In Rice Production, And India Became A Food Grain (Rice And Wheat) Surplus Country. This Achievement Was Primarily Attributed To Modernization Of Traditional Practices With The Use Of High Yielding Varieties Of Seeds, Chemical Fertilizers And Pesticides. But About Forty Years After, It Was Found That Crop Productivity And Production Has Been Affected Adversely. There Is Great Loss Of Soil Fertility And Biodiversity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Organic Rice Protein cause Bloating?

So Far, There Have Been No Complaints About Organic Rice Protein Causing Bloating. Usually, Bloating Is Caused By An Abrupt Increase In Fiber Intake. But Because Fiber Is Taken Out From The Rice During Rice Protein Processing, You Can Expect Little To No Bloating. Plus, Rice Is A Hypoallergenic Food.

Can I use Organic Rice Protein for Weight Loss?

Protein, In General, Is Great For Weight Loss. Protein-Rich Food Can Help You Feel Full Longer, Allowing You To Curb Your Hunger And Control Your Food Intake. So, Yes, You Can Use Organic Rice Protein To Aid Your Weight Loss.

How does organic rice protein taste like?

You Can Choose Among A Wide Variety Of Flavors, From Chocolate To Vanilla To Strawberry. But In General, Organic Rice Protein Has A Bland Flavor With A Chalky Texture. If Youre Considering To Purchase Organic Rice Protein Shakes, Try Samples First Before Investing In Big Tubs Of Rice Protein Powder.
